Seaside Resort

A modern beachfront resort offering a relaxed yet luxurious atmosphere. Surrounded by pools, palm trees, and ocean views, it creates the perfect balance between calm and energy. Guests can enjoy private beach access, sunset lounges, and world-class dining experiences just steps away from their rooms. Whether you're looking to recharge during the day or get ready for unforgettable nights, this resort offers the ideal festival getaway.

An elegant hotel with a warm and intimate courtyard setting. Designed for comfort and social moments, it provides a peaceful escape from the festival crowds. The charming architecture, cozy outdoor spaces, and personalized service make it perfect for those who appreciate a more relaxed and stylish atmosphere. Enjoy quiet mornings, refreshing drinks in the courtyard, and a calm retreat after vibrant festival nights.

A contemporary hillside hotel with panoramic views over the sea and surrounding area. Perfect for guests who want privacy, comfort, and luxury. The spacious rooms, infinity pools, and rooftop terraces create a truly exclusive experience. Wake up to breathtaking views, unwind in a serene environment, and enjoy premium amenities while still being just minutes away from the festival.
